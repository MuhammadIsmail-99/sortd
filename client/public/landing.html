How it works

Three steps. Zero effort.

No more screenshots you never look at. Sortd turns any link into structured knowledge in seconds.

1

Paste a link

Any YouTube Short, Instagram Reel, TikTok, or regular web URL. Just paste and hit send — from any browser or app.

2

AI reads it

Whisper transcribes the audio. Gemini summarizes, categorizes, and extracts the key ideas in seconds.

3

It's organized

Auto-filed into your collections. Full-text searchable. Available forever, right when you need it.
